# Introduction

## What is Richie.js?

**Richie.js** is a cutting-edge **SEO toolkit** designed to simplify and automate the creation of structured data for **20 rich result types**, helping your website achieve better rankings on [Google](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/search-gallery). These **rich snippets** are visually engaging and provide users with actionable, detailed information directly in search results.

Built with modern technologies like **Node.js** and **JavaScript**, Richie.js transforms the complex process of structured data generation into an effortless task.

With Richie.js, you can:

- Automatically **fetch data** from HTML sources.
- Convert content into **Google-compliant structured data**.
- Generate **JSON-LD output** that integrates seamlessly with search engines.

Whether you're an **SEO expert**, **developer**, or **digital marketer**, Richie.js empowers you to maximize your site's search engine performance without the usual hassle.
